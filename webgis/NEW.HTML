Our Core GIS Services

We support organizations that need location-based intelligence. Whether you are trying to trace electricity losses, monitor disease hotspots, or plan resilient cities, our team designs practical GIS solutions using open-source and enterprise tools.

Utilities

Electricity Network Mapping

We map primary and secondary substations, feeders, and customer meters to help utilities understand technical and commercial losses, plan maintenance, and improve reliability.

Health

Health & Disease Surveillance

Using GIS with R and AI, we help public health teams visualize outbreaks, identify hotspots, and plan rapid responses to protect communities.

Cities

Urban Planning & SDG Dashboards

We support city authorities to visualize land use, services, and infrastructure, and to track SDG indicators in intuitive web dashboards.
